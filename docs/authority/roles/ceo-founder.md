# CEO/CTO & Founder - Josh

**Status:** Active
**Filled by:** Human
**Reports to:** Nobody

## What This Role Does

Vision, technical exploration, creative direction. The person who sees where things should go and builds the hard parts. Generates ideas constantly - that's a feature, not a bug, but it needs a counterweight.

## Current Responsibilities

- Technical architecture decisions
- Hands-on coding (the deep, hard stuff)
- Product vision and direction
- External relationships (content, social, clients)
- Final approval on anything high-risk

## What This Role Needs From the Org

- Someone to keep the product focused when ideas scatter
- Systems that run without babysitting
- Honest pushback when an idea is premature or off-track
- A team that builds itself out over time

## Signals This Role Generates

- Ideas (via Discord `/idea`, direct conversation, or board)
- Approvals/rejections on proposals
- Technical direction changes
- Revenue and business strategy

## Load Indicators

When this role is overloaded:

- Ideas pile up without execution
- Context switching between too many threads
- No time for the creative/exploratory work that drives everything

## Evolution

This role stays human. The goal is to offload everything that isn't creative vision and deep technical work to the AI team.
